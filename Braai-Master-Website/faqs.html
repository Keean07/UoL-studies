Our motto towards newcomers:

Guardian, we've seen the furthest reachest of the galaxy, we've touched both the light and the dark and prevailed through both.

With that being said, we understand that sometimes the darkest places can reside within ourselves - and our most powerful enemy can often be fear.

We know that not everyone is comfortable with sharing their voice. Some people need extra time, others need some motivation, but regardless of who you are or what you need, we are prepared to walk with you, in whichever way you feel most comfortable, until the day comes that you are ready to show the world your face.

At the end of the day, the most important thing to us is that all players get to experience the richness of a gaming community where they feel safe and accepted.

Once that feeling of security sinks in, we can begin to enjoy all aspects of gaming that was once unreachable to us.

So reach out, join us, let us fight, guardian. We are stronger together.
